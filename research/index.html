Energy Consumption

Full Title: Analytics of energy consumption behavior
Dec 2016 - Apr 2017
Main Goal: Data analytics performed over energy consumption data from Commercial & Industrial Clients requested by one of the most bigggest utilities companies in Colombia.
Tech Stack: R, Python (numpy, pandas, scikit-learn), Oracle DB
• Led the development of 3 research lines: (1) Energy consumption patterns (2) Risk level qualification and (3) Energy consumption forecasting.
• Use of AI and ML techniques: Clustering (hierarchical and K-means), Dimensionality reduction (PCA and t-SNE) and ARIMA modeling.
• Led the documentation of project deliverables (incrementals and final).
